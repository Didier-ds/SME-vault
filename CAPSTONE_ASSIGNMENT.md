# Capstone Project Definition & Market Analysis
## SME Vault: Non-Custodial Crypto Treasury Management for SMEs

---

## Part A: Final Project Proposal

### 1. Initial Project Overview

SME Vault is a non-custodial, smart contract-based treasury management system built on Solana that enables small and medium enterprises (SMEs) to manage their cryptocurrency funds with enterprise-grade security controls. The platform provides multi-approval workflows, programmable spending limits, time-delayed withdrawals for large amounts, and emergency freeze capabilities—all enforced on-chain without requiring third-party custody. Unlike enterprise solutions that cost $10,000+ per month, SME Vault offers these controls at an affordable $50/month, making sophisticated treasury management accessible to businesses with 10-500 employees that are already using or considering crypto for payments and treasury operations.

---

### 2. Core Value Proposition & Product-Market Fit (PMF)

**Value Proposition:**

SME Vault gives small businesses the treasury controls of a Fortune 500 company at a fraction of the cost, fully on-chain. We solve the critical security gap for SMEs managing crypto: traditional wallets are single points of failure with no approval workflows, while enterprise solutions are prohibitively expensive. Our non-custodial smart contract platform enforces multi-approval workflows, spending limits, time delays, and emergency controls that cannot be bypassed, providing bank-level security without trusting third parties.

**Product-Market Fit Analysis:**

The product addresses a clear market need: SMEs increasingly hold crypto for international payments, treasury diversification, and operational expenses, but lack appropriate security controls. The market fit is validated by three key factors: (1) **Affordability gap**: Existing enterprise solutions (Fireblocks, Copper) cost $10K+/month, pricing out SMEs, (2) **Security necessity**: High-profile crypto thefts and operational errors demonstrate the need for multi-approval workflows, and (3) **Regulatory readiness**: As crypto adoption grows, businesses need audit trails and compliance controls that basic wallets cannot provide. Our solution sits in the "Goldilocks zone"—more secure than basic wallets, more affordable than enterprise solutions, and fully non-custodial unlike custodial services.

### 4. Competitor Landscape

#### Direct Competitors

1. **Gnosis Safe (Ethereum/Polygon)**
   - Strengths: Established brand, multi-chain support, large user base
   - Weaknesses: No programmable rules/limits, no time delays, Ethereum gas costs, complex UX for non-technical users
   - Gap: Lacks the programmable security controls that SMEs need

2. **Fireblocks (Enterprise)**
   - Strengths: Comprehensive security, insurance, compliance features
   - Weaknesses: $10K+/month pricing, requires custody, overkill for SMEs
   - Gap: Pricing and complexity exclude small businesses

3. **Copper.co (Enterprise)**
   - Strengths: Institutional-grade security, regulatory compliance
   - Weaknesses: Enterprise-only pricing, custodial model, complex onboarding
   - Gap: Not accessible to SMEs

#### Indirect Competitors

4. **MetaMask/Phantom (Basic Wallets)**
   - Strengths: Free, easy to use, widely adopted
   - Weaknesses: Single point of failure, no approval workflows, no limits
   - Gap: Zero business controls

5. **Multis (Ethereum)**
   - Strengths: Business-focused, good UX
   - Weaknesses: Ethereum-only, gas costs, limited programmable rules
   - Gap: Not on Solana (lower fees), less programmable

6. **BitGo (Custodial)**
   - Strengths: Insurance, compliance, established
   - Weaknesses: Custodial (requires trust), enterprise pricing
   - Gap: Trust model and pricing exclude SMEs

#### Competitive Advantages

- **Solana-based**: Lower transaction fees ($0.00025 vs $5-50 on Ethereum)
- **Programmable rules**: On-chain limits, delays, and thresholds that cannot be bypassed
- **Non-custodial**: Users maintain full control, no third-party risk
- **Affordable**: $50/month vs $10K+/month for enterprise solutions
- **SME-focused**: Built specifically for small businesses, not adapted from enterprise

#### Research Notes

**AI-Identified Competitors:** Gnosis Safe, Fireblocks, Copper.co, MetaMask, Multis
**Manually Discovered Competitors:** BitGo, Coinbase Custody, Anchorage (all custodial/enterprise), Rainbow (basic wallet), Argent (smart wallet but limited business features)

**Key Insight:** The AI correctly identified major players but missed that most competitors fall into two categories: (1) Basic wallets with no business controls, or (2) Enterprise solutions that are too expensive. The "middle market" of affordable, programmable, non-custodial solutions is largely underserved.
