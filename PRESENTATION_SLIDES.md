## Slide 2: The Problem
**Title:** "The Security Gap in Crypto Treasury Management"

**Content:**
- **Bullet Points:**

  - No approval workflows—anyone with the key can move unlimited funds
  - Zero fraud prevention—no way to detect or stop suspicious transactions
  - Enterprise solutions cost $10K+/month (unaffordable for SMEs)
  - Custodial services require trusting third parties

**Visual:**

- Red X or warning symbols
- Maybe a comparison chart showing cost gap

**Design Notes:**
- Use red/orange for problem indicators

## Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme
- **Primary:** Professional blue or green (trust/security)
- **Accent:** Orange/red for warnings, green for success
- **Background:** White or light gray
- **Text:** Dark gray or black

### Typography
- **Headings:** Bold, 32-40pt
- **Body:** Regular, 18-24pt
- **Bullets:** 16-18pt
- **Font:** Sans-serif (Arial, Helvetica, or similar)

### Visual Elements
- Use icons consistently (Lucide, Font Awesome, or similar)
- Keep diagrams simple and clear
- Use high-contrast colors for readability
- Limit text per slide (6-7 bullets max)

### Slide Transitions
- Keep transitions subtle (fade or slide)
- Don't overuse animations
- Ensure smooth transitions between slides
